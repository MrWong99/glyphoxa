Mascot and banner concepts for **Glyphoxa** — an AI-powered voice NPC framework for tabletop RPGs.

### 1. `mascot-golem.png` — Stone Golem Mascot
A friendly magical stone golem/familiar with glowing blue runic glyph markings carved into its body. It's speaking (with visible sound waves) and sits on a tabletop RPG battle map with dice nearby. Designed as a project mascot.

**Use:** Avatar, social previews, documentation mascot.

The word "GLYPHOXA" in an ornate fantasy font with runic decorations, a d20 die, and magical energy. Includes the tagline "AI Voice NPCs for Tabletop RPGs." Purple, gold, and cyan color scheme on dark background.

**Use:** GitHub README header, repository social preview, website banner.

### 3. `gopher-dm.png` — Go Gopher as Dungeon Master
The Go gopher wearing a DM cloak and wizard hat with glowing runes, holding a microphone and a d20 die. Magical sound waves emanate from its mouth. Playful and fun.

**Use:** Go community posts, talks, documentation for Go developers.

### 4. `abstract-waves-runes.png` — Sound Waves to Runes
Abstract art showing audio waveforms morphing into magical glyphs and runes (left to right). Electric blue waves transition to warm gold/amber runes. Represents the fusion of AI voice tech and fantasy magic.

**Use:** Wide banner, presentation slides, website hero section.
